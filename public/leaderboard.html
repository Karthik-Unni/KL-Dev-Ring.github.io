03 / RANKING ENGINE

How the score works.

Caps prevent any single metric from dominating. There are no followers, likes, or page views. The formula is public and versioned.

Read the spec ↗
COMMITS log2(n + 1) × 18 Logarithmic — sustained output over spikes
MERGED PRs min(n, 75) × 1.8 Capped at 75 PRs
PROJECTS min(n, 20) × 5 Capped at 20 shipped projects
POSTS min(n, 40) × 2.2 Capped at 40 published posts
STREAK (weeks) min(n, 52) × 1.5 Capped at 52 weeks
TOTAL BUILDER SCORE Σ above Integer, publicly auditable
